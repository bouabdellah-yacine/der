The only seven -time Ballon d'Or and six -time Golden Boot player , Messi is considered one of the best football players of any generation. The most decisive player of the 21st century , top scorer in a single club shirt, he was voted best right winger of all time by France Football while the IFFHS named him best player of the decade from 2011 to 2020 . Messi started football in his hometown of Rosario in Argentina with clubs Grandoli and Newell's Old Boys . Suffering from a growth problem, he joined FC Barcelona at the age of thirteen , which financed his hormonal treatment. An emblematic player of this club, of which he is notably the most capped player, he remained there for sixteen professional years before joining Paris Saint-Germain in 2021 to continue his career. A creative and complete player, as well as a scorer, leader and organizer of the game, he holds many records: eight-time winner of the Pichichi , he is the top scorer in the history of the Spanish championship , FC Barcelona , ​​the Supercopa de Spain , the European Super Cup , the Argentina selection , the South American selections and the second top scorer in the Champions League . Author of more than 750 career goals and involved in more than 1,000 goals, he is according to the IFFHS the second highest scorer of all time in official matches.. He also holds the world record for most goals scored in a season , in a calendar year, and the record for goals in a season in a European league. He is in another register the player with the most official assists recorded in the history of football.
